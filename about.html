FASHION
DESIGNER

A designer specializing in heritage menswear, apparel construction, and technical design.

Mathew Guzman (b. Mission Viejo, CA) approaches fashion with a focus on functional, high-quality garments. Currently a student at ASU FIDM, he explores the intersection of vintage ivy style and modern Americana.

With a background in luxury retail styling at Nordstrom, Mathew understands the bridge between creative concept and the consumer experience.

Currently developing the "Uniform" capstone collection, he continues to refine his vision of an ideal heritage wardrobe.
